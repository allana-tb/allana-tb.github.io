About me

I’m a Masters student in Computer Science at UFMG and I’m also studying Systems Analysis and Development at PUC Minas. My main research interests are Complex Systems, Optimization and Social Computing!

I’ve always really liked Maths, Humanities and interdisciplinary topics. Because of that, I became a bachelor in Administration. During my graduation, though, I had the opportunity to be a junior research fellow in Network Science, applying it in a real-world bidding processes. That lead me to also discover programming, starting with R. But that was it, I was absolutely hooked!

A data and coding enthusiast, I'm usually reading or watching something - and sometimes baking during my free time.
