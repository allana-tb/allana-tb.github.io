First steps

My journey with researching started back in high school, in 2015, when I was a Junior Research Fellow Volunteer at CEFET-MG. I worked on enumerability and its consequential effects on infinity. Even without a direct application back then, I really enjoyed researching about this topic. It was a pleasant surprise that this mathematical property has such a significant influence on Computer Science!

When I started my graduation in Administration, I already knew that research was something I was keen on doing. In my first semester, I got into a research group, PET in Administration. It is a federal program that focuses on Research, Extension and Teaching - the basis of universities in Brazil. Taking part in a self-organized group with several demands in projects of these three areas was a great opportunity. I honed my interpersonal skills as well as my time management skills.
